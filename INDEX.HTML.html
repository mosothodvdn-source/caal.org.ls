Home

Welcome to the official website of the Clinical Anaesthetists Association of Lesotho. Our mission is to uphold high‑quality, ethical, and holistic anaesthetic practice across the nation, guided by both local and international standards.

Vision

To promote strong ethical standards and create an environment that empowers anaesthetists to deliver the best possible care.

Mission

To ensure high‑quality, holistic anaesthetic practice without discrimination, in line with guidelines regulating anaesthesia practice.

Membership

Membership is open to all clinical practitioners with anaesthetic qualifications who are registered with any health professions council in Lesotho.

  • Apply for membership and await approval from the Executive Committee.
  • Pay an annual subscription fee as determined by the Executive Committee.
  • Membership may be terminated for breach of the Code of Conduct or failure to pay subscription fees.
