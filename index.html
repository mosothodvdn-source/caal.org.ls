Welcome

Welcome to the official website of CAAL, a professional body representing anaesthesia providers in Lesotho. We promote safe, ethical, and evidence-based anaesthesia practice aligned with international standards.

About CAAL

CAAL is a national professional association dedicated to advancing anaesthesia care through education, advocacy, and collaboration. We work closely with national and international stakeholders to improve patient safety and professional development.
